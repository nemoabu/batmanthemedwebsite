Characters

Batman / Bruce Wayne

The main character as expected is Batman, Bruce Wayne dedicated his life fighting crime as the Batman. Bruce Wayne is a billionaire who philanthropist who witnessed the murder of his parents in Gotham. Haunted by this past and driven by justice Batman uses his intellect, combat abilities, and technology to project Gotham from the most dangerous threats.

Joker

Mastermind joker with his chaotic criminal abilities stands as Batman’s most infamous enemy. With his twisted since of human and his unpredictability the Joker seeks to create anarchy in Gotham City. Joker ends up challenging batman both physically and psychologically embodying the dark aspects of human nature.

 Harvey Dent / Two-Face

Initially being Gotham’s honored District Attorney, after his tragic scarring during confrontation with criminals has led him to madness. Adopting the person of Two-Face he becomes obsessed with duality and characters flipping a coin to determine his actions.

Rachel Dawes

Rachel Dawes is childhood friend of Bruce Wayne a passionate assistant district attorney. She serves as Bruce’s moral anchor, helping Batman confront his inner demons and inspire him push forward. Rachel’s tragic fate deeply impacts Bruce and allows him to push beyond his capabilities.

Alfred Pennyworth

Alfred is Bruce’s butler who is loyal and confident with Batman’s decisions. Alfred provides support in many realms such as emotional, practical, as well as guidance to batman in his journey against crime. Like Rachel Alfred serves as Bruce’s moral compass offering perspective during Bruce’s movements of doubt and despair.
