Plot

In "Batman: The Dark Knight," Bruce Wayne, haunted by the murder of his parents, becomes the masked vigilante Batman, dedicated to protecting Gotham City. As Batman faces off against the criminal underworld, he encounters his greatest adversary, the enigmatic and chaotic Joker, whose reign of terror threatens to plunge the city into chaos. Alongside allies like Commissioner Gordon and butler Alfred, Batman must navigate the treacherous landscape of Gotham while confronting his own inner demons.
